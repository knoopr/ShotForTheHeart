About

Shot for the heart is a semester long campus wide game of tag with the goal of raising funds for the Heart and Stroke foundation of Canada. The way the game works is that for every $20 you raise you are given a new piece of information about your "target" as you unlock more pieced of information it becomes easier to find your target. The last 2 people in the game are given prizes.
